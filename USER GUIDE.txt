PHP Task Management Application - User Guide


Adding a Task:
Tasks can be added to the list in two ways:

##Click on "Add Task" Button: Enter the task in the input field and click the "Add Task" button.


##Press Enter Key: Type the task in the input field and press the "Enter" key on your keyboard.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Completing a Task:

To mark a task as completed, click on the "Complete" button next to the task. The task will be marked as completed, and a checkmark will appear in the checkbox.


Deleting a Task:
To remove a task from the list, click on the "Delete" button next to the task. The task will be deleted and will no longer appear in the list.


Task Persistence:

Tasks are stored in a database, ensuring that they persist even if the page is refreshed, or if the user closes and reopens the website.
Completed tasks and their status are also saved, allowing users to track completed tasks over multiple sessions.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Task Display:

Tasks are displayed in a list format, showing the task name, a checkbox indicating completion status, and buttons for completing and deleting tasks.
Completed tasks are indicated by a checkmark in the checkbox.
Tasks are listed in the order they were added.
